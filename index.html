Hire Me Tho

I had the unique burden of really supportive parents. You know how artists can be. I spent most of my life juggling fantasies about being Indiana Jones or Michael Jackson. So I took the scenic route. Now I have student loans AND a penchant for R&B.
Naturally, I received my B.A. in Anthropology and played music in bands for most of my twenties. It went. We opened up for several, two - maybe, national acts, Walk the Moon (RCA) and the Idle Hands. The Idle Hands opened up for us, yes technically we paid them. We put some music on TV too, then did the legal battle thing.

Scene swipe here, so as the sun began to set on my twenties, I pulled an audible. Short story is I got into coding. It forced me to understand systems more clearly than ever before. I think it actually made me a better person too but I'm probably just being self indulgent. Straight up, programming is the perfect union between technical proficiency and creative intuition. BINGO. BANGO.

That was my parents exhaling. Life's short. Wear a lot of hats. Mike and Indie did.
